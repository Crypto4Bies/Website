What is an NFT?

So what is an NFT? NFT stands for non-fungible token, put simply it is an image or 'artwork' that is unique and of perspective value. However, we aim to use the Crypto4Bie's NFT's as a ticket into our community. Meaning that only NFT holders will have access to our benefits, utilities and exclusive channels on discord.
